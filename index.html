How to play

Click "Roll dice". Then type as many words as you can in the time limit, using the keyboard. You only need a maximum of 5 words of each length.

Rules

Perquackey is a game for 2 or more players. Open one copy of this tab for each player. Each player takes a turn, then the next player plays, and so on. You get 3 minutes to make words. Words must be: At least 3 letters No proper nouns For "s" plurals: you can use "dog" or "dogs", but not both. After the round ends, you will receive a score based on how many words of each length you spelled. Once you reach 2000 points, you become "vulnerable". You can no longer form 3-letter words. Also, if you score under 500 in a round, you receive in penalty. But, you get three extra red dice each round. The first player to 5000 points wins.
